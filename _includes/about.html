About Renova

The University of Colorado - Boulder's New Music Choir

We are Renova!

We are students and alumni of the University of Colorado committed to bringing new choral music to life. Guided by our masterful director, Jessie Flasschoen-Campbell, we sing the newest (and in our opinion, greatest) music of young composers.


If you would like to join us, send us an email or audition!
