And you! If you have a piece you would like us to consider singing, please submit this form.


Submission Criteria

  • 1. The piece must have no larger divisis than 8-part SSAATTBB, and adhere to reasonable voice ranges. (Note that non-SATB music is also acceptable, such as SAA, TTB+Piano, etc.)

  • 2. The piece must not exceed 6 minutes in length.

  • 3. Any text must either be original, public domain, or used with express permission of the writer.

  • 4. You must submit a PDF score of the full, complete piece.

  • 5. You must submit an audio file of the full score. MIDI audio is acceptable.

  • 6. Scores that appear disorganized, unprepared, or incomplete will not be accepted. For tips and tricks on formatting and style for choral music, visit this helpful link.
